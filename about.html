My day-to-day work covers Microsoft 365, Intune, Entra ID, support, troubleshooting and project delivery. I like practical IT, clear documentation, and making things work properly.

Hobbies & interests

Building PCs

I enjoy both the planning side and the hands-on side of PC building. From picking parts to cable routing and final setup, it’s the kind of project I genuinely like getting stuck into.

There’s something satisfying about taking a pile of components and ending up with a clean, working system.

Warhammer 40K

Warhammer is one of the hobbies I spend the most time on. I like the full mix of building, painting, collecting and actually playing games.

It scratches both the detail-focused side and the more social side of things.

Painting & modelling

I also enjoy the slower hobby side of Warhammer. Painting models is a nice change of pace from screen time and gives me something creative to focus on.

Board games

I’m a fan of board games that are social, strategic and just competitive enough. They’re a good excuse to get people together and switch off for a while.

Family

A lot of my free time is spent with family, whether that’s trips out, holidays or just making time to do things together.

Friends

I also spend plenty of time with friends, whether that’s travelling, going out, gaming, or just hanging out. That balance is important to me.

Climbing

Climbing is another hobby I enjoy, mainly because it has that same problem-solving side I like in other things. It’s a good way to switch off, stay active and focus on something completely different from work.
