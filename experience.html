Case Studies

SolidWorks PDM Migration to Azure

Environment: On-prem → Azure VPC

Problem: Clients could not connect to the PDM service despite successful RDP and network access.

What I did: Led troubleshooting across internal teams and third-party support. Analysed device configuration and network traffic using Wireshark and Intune policy review.

Outcome: Identified a firewall policy mismatch causing inconsistent behaviour between wired and wireless connections and restored full service connectivity.

Microsoft 365 Tenant Migration (75 Devices)

Environment: Hybrid → Entra ID (cloud-only)

Problem: Required migration of all user devices to a new Microsoft 365 tenant following consolidation.

What I did: Created a full migration plan including device audit, lifecycle assessment, and user scheduling. Rebuilt and enrolled devices using Intune and OneDrive backups.

Outcome: Migrated ~75 devices, decommissioned outdated hardware, and completed the rollout within 4 months.

Mobile Device Management (iOS & Android)

Environment: Intune, Apple Business Manager

Problem: Required structured mobile deployment across two organisations within a single tenant.

What I did: Designed enrolment profiles for Android (fully managed & BYOD) and configured Apple Business Manager for automated iOS deployment.

Outcome: Delivered a scalable and automated mobile device management platform with improved security and consistency.

Helpdesk System & Workflow Implementation

Environment: Microsoft 365, MSP Manager

Problem: Legacy ticketing relied on manual email-based processes.

What I did: Implemented a new helpdesk platform, configured SLAs and dashboards, and built a Microsoft Forms + Power Automate workflow for structured ticket creation.

Outcome: Delivered a fully functional ticketing system with improved visibility, automation, and user adoption.
